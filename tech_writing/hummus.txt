<task id="Making_Hummus">
	<title>Making traditional hummus</title>
	<shortdesc>Hummus is a simple yet delicious side dish that only requires a few ingredients.
	It is also great for anyone who is vegetarian or vegan.</shortdesc>
	
	<taskbody>
	<context>This recipe instructs you how to make your own hummus in just a few short 
	minutes.<p/></context>
	
	<steps>
		<step>
	<cmd>First, gather all the necessary ingredients: 2 cloves garlic, 1 8oz. can garbanzo beans
	(chickpeas), 1/4 cup tahini (sesame paste), 1 tsp. salt, 3 tbsp. fresh lemon juice, smoked
	paprika for garnish (optional), fresh pita for serving (optional). </cmd>
		</step>
		<step>
	<cmd>Add the can of garbanzos to a food processor, draining and reserving excess liquid.
	This can be used to thin out the hummus at the end if it's too thick.</cmd>
		</step>
		<step>
	<cmd>Mince the garlic finely and add to the food processor. Then add the tahini, salt,
	and lemon juice.</cmd>
		</step>
		<step>
	<cmd>Blend until smooth. If the hummus is too thick add some of the reserve bean liquid.</cmd>
		</step>
		<step>
	<cmd>Sprinkle paprika or olive oil over the hummus before serving (optional). Serve
	with chips, warm pita, or vegetables. Enjoy!</cmd>
		</step>
	</steps>
	</taskbody>
</task>
